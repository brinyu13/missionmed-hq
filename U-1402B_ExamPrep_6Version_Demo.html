How are the live drilling sessions structured? v
Sessions run in small groups with a trained instructor. You work through clinical reasoning questions in real time, talk through your thought process out loud, and receive immediate feedback. Every session targets specific reasoning patterns.
Is this for Step 1, Step 2 CK, or both? v
We offer structured training paths for both Step 1 and Step 2 CK. The core reasoning methodology applies to both, with exam-specific question patterns and content focus for each.
How is this different from question banks? v
Question banks give you questions and explanations. We train the reasoning process itself. The difference is like reading about swimming versus getting in the pool with a coach. You practice thinking through problems in real time, not just reviewing answers.
