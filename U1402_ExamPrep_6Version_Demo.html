Questions Students Usually Ask

What if I already have a question bank?
Good. Keep using it. MissionMed does not replace your question bank. It trains you to reason through those questions differently. The question bank gives you content. We train the skill of applying it under pressure.
How is this different from watching videos?
Videos are passive. Drilling is active. When you watch a video, your brain absorbs information. When you drill, your brain practices using it under time pressure. That is the difference between knowing and performing.
Do I need a strong content foundation first?
You need a basic foundation, yes. But most students who are stuck already know enough content. The problem is not what they know. It is how they apply it. We start working with your existing knowledge and fill specific gaps as we drill.
What is the difference between Team Drilling and 1-on-1?
Team Drilling builds speed and resilience through group pressure. 1-on-1 Tutoring diagnoses and fixes your individual reasoning gaps. Some students do both. Some start with one and switch. We help you decide which fits your situation.
