Mission Briefing

When should I start working with Mission Residency? +
The best time is as early as possible in your application cycle. Many students start 6 to 12 months before ERAS opens. However, even if you are late in the cycle or reapplying, a strategic approach can still make a significant difference.
I have a low Step score. Is it too late for me? +
A low score does not automatically disqualify you. Programs evaluate many factors beyond test scores. Strategy, interview performance, and how you present your application can shift the outcome significantly. That is exactly what we help you build.
What if I have already gone unmatched? +
Reapplicants are one of the groups we work with most frequently. Going unmatched is not the end. It means you need a different strategy, not more of the same.
How is pricing handled? +
Pricing is discussed during your strategy session. We want to understand your situation, your goals, and your timeline before recommending a specific program tier.
